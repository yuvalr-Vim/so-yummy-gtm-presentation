The Scrappy Brand Deal Hack

"Our first brand deals were FREE"

The Problem

We wanted brands to pay us for content.

But no brand wanted to be FIRST.

They all asked: "Who else have you worked with?"

Our Hack

  1. Reached out to 3 good brand names
  2. Offered to feature them FOR FREE
  3. They said yes (free advertising for them!)
  4. Now we could say "We've worked with X, Y, Z"
  5. Other brands wanted to PAY us

üí° Lesson: Sometimes you have to give value first to GET value later. Don't be afraid to work for free at the beginning to build credibility.
